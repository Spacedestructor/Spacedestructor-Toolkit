TwitchToolkit

TwitchToolkit is a mod written by Twitch.tv/hodlhodl that integrates storytelling decisions into chat votes, awards viewers coins for watching, and those coins can be spent on items/events in game.

  • !bal - Shows how much Coins you have, your Karma and how much you gain every Coin Cycle
  • !whatiskarma - Explains how Karma works
  • !purchaselist - Gives you the Link to this Page
  • !giftcoins - Ussage: !giftcoins username ammount
  • !joinqueue - Lets you join the Name Queue
  • !modinfo - Explains the Twitch integration
  • !buy - Usage: !buy itemname/eventname
  • !levelskill Usage: !levelskill skillname ammount Keep in mind that Ammount is reffering to how much coins your spending on it not the amount of XP you will get
  • !modsettings - Shows if Coin gain and the Store are enabled as well as how much Coins you gain, how long a Coin Cycle is and the Karma cap
  • !instructions - Also explains the Twitch integration but also contains a link to this Page
  • !allcommands - Shows you a list of all Commands aviable to you, mods will have some extra Commands
  • !installedmods - Shows you what Mods are currently running. Warning: This WILL generate a Wall of Text in Chat! (16 Messages will be send)
  • !reviveall - Revives all Dead Colonists
  • !reviveme - Reives your Colonist
  • !healme - Heals your Colonist
  • !healall - Heals all Colonists
  • !passionshuffle - Shuffles all Passions of the Colonist ammount of Minor and Major Passions Stays the same
  • !surgery
  • !addtrait traitname - Adds a Trait to your Colonist
  • !removetrait traitname - Removes a Trait from your Colonist
  • !reviveanypawn - Revives a Random Colonist
  • !healanypawn - Heals a Random Colonist
  • !replacetrait firsttrait second trait - Replaces the first Trait with the second Trait
  • !backpack - ???
  • !removepassion - Removes a Poassion from your Colonist
  • !addpassion - Adds a Passion to your Colonist
  • !fullheal - Heals all Injurys of your Colonist
  • !rescueme - ???
  • !mypawnkills - Shows the Kill Count of your Colonist
  • !mypawnstats - Shows Combat related Stats of your Colonist
  • !mypawnhealth - Shows the Health of your Colonist
  • !mypawnneeds - Shows the Needs of your Colonist
  • !mypawngear - Shows the Gear of your Colonist
  • !mypawnbody - Shows Implants, replaced Body Parts as well as Health conditions of your Colonist
  • !mypawnwork - Shows the Work priorities of your Colonist
  • !mypawnstory - Shows the Backstory, Traits and Gender of your Colonist
  • !mypawnskills - Shows the Skill Level and the Passions of your Colonist
  • !fixmypawn - If the Twitch integration says your not Linked anymore, this might fix it
  • !leave - Makes your Colonist leave the Colony without Mood debuff
  • !mypawnrelations - Shows the Relationships of your Colonist
  • !factions - Shows a list of all Factions and there Opinion
  • !research - shows current Research and the Progress
  • !lookup - Usage: !lookup category name lists the Search results for your category name and item name input
  • !price - Usage: !price itemname/eventname Shows how much something costs
  • !insul - ???

  • !colonists - Shows how many Pawns are in the Colony including dead unburied Colonists
